Frequently Asked Questions



1. How do I rent an outfit from Outfitters?

Selecting your perfect style from our collection is a breeze. Simply browse through our catalog, choose the outfit you love, and book it for your desired duration.

2. What are the rental durations available?

You can rent outfits for 3, 5, 7, or 10 days. This way, you have the flexibility to match the outfit with your occasion.

3. Is the outfit cleaned and altered before delivery?

Absolutely! Each outfit is meticulously cleaned and professionally altered to your size before it's delivered to your doorstep.

4. What if the outfit doesn't fit or I'm not satisfied?

We understand the importance of a perfect fit. If there are any issues, contact us within 24 hours of receiving the outfit, and we'll arrange for an exchange or a refund.

5. Can influencers collaborate with designers?

Absolutely! Influencers with a good reach are welcome to request collaborations with our talented designers. Together, we can create something extraordinary.

6. Can I extend the rental duration?

Yes, you can extend the rental duration. Just reach out to us, and we'll make the necessary arrangements.

7. How do I return the outfit?

Packing the outfit for return is hassle-free. We'll provide all the necessary instructions and arrange for a convenient pickup after your occasion.
